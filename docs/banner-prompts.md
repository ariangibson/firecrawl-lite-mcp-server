# Banner image prompts (Nano Banana Pro / Gemini 3 Pro Image)

Generate at a wide **3:1 aspect ratio** (e.g. 1500×500, or 2400×800 for retina).
Save the chosen result as `docs/banner.png` — the README references it at the top.

Each prompt renders the title text directly in the banner. If you prefer a
text-free graphic, delete the "Render the text…" sentence and keep the H1 in
the README. If you keep the text in the image, you can remove the `# Firecrawl
Lite MCP Server` heading from the README to avoid duplication.

---

> A premium, wide 3:1 technical hero banner for a developer tool. Deep charcoal-to-midnight-slate background with a subtle dot-grid and faint floating lines of monospace code. On the right two-thirds, a sleek stylized "web crawler" formed from glowing embers and circuit traces — a spider-like spark of energy emerging from an abstract browser window, trailing molten orange particles that cool into violet. Soft volumetric glow, crisp rim lighting, shallow depth of field, high detail, modern SaaS aesthetic in the style of Vercel and Linear marketing art. On the left third, render the text "Firecrawl Lite" as a bold clean geometric sans-serif in near-white, with the smaller tagline "Privacy-first web scraping for MCP" beneath it in muted grey. Balanced negative space, no clutter. Color palette: charcoal, ember orange (#FF6A2B), electric violet (#7C5CFF), off-white. Avoid: photographic faces, stock-photo people, busy backgrounds, watermarks, distorted text.
